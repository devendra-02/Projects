About Amar Punjabi

A True Gem of Vegetarian Hospitality

Amar Punjabi Foods & Amar Punjabi Dhaba are proud units of the prestigious Amar Punjabi Group, a celebrated name in the world of vegetarian dining in Kota. With a legacy of more than five decades, Amar Punjabi has become a true landmark of taste and tradition, serving authentic Punjabi and Rajasthani cuisine. Guided by the belief that “Purity of Food reflects Purity of Mind,” we have always strived to deliver food that is not only rich in flavor and aroma but also in quality, love, and hospitality.

Branches of Amar Punjabi

Amar Punjabi Foods

Taste. Tradition. Together.

Amar Punjabi Foods is a pure vegetarian family restaurant in Talwandi, Kota. We serve a wide variety of Punjabi, Rajasthani, North Indian, and Chinese cuisines. Our specialties like Dal Butter Fry, Paneer Taka Tak, Paneer Butter Masala, and Fruit Raita make us a favorite among students and families. Dine-in, takeaway, and home delivery options are available.
